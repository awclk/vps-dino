FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What operating systems are supported?
We support a wide range of Linux distributions including Ubuntu, CentOS, Debian, and Fedora. Windows Server is also available for an additional license fee.
Can I upgrade my VPS later?
Yes, you can upgrade your resources at any time through your control panel. Upgrades typically take effect within minutes with no downtime.
Do you offer backups?
Automatic daily backups are available as an add-on service. We also provide snapshot functionality for point-in-time recovery.
What's your uptime guarantee?
We offer a 99.9% uptime SLA for all our VPS plans. If we fall short, you'll receive service credits per our terms.
How is billing handled?
All plans are billed monthly. Annual payment options are available with a 20% discount. You can cancel at any time.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all new VPS accounts. Domain registrations and add-ons are non-refundable.
